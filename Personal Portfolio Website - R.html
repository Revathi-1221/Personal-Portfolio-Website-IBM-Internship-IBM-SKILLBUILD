    AI-Enhanced Traffic Monitoring Using Object Detection In Images

    ONITERING SYSTEM This paper presents an AI-enhanced traffic monitoring system that utilizes advanced object detection techniques to analyze vehicular activity from image and video data. Leveraging deep learning models such as YOLO (You Only Look Once) and SSD (Single Shot MultiBox Detector), the system accurately identifies and tracks vehicles, pedestrians, and other road elements in real time. The proposed approach offers a scalable and cost-effective alternative to traditional traffic monitoring systems by automating data collection and analysis. Applications include traffic flow optimization, congestion detection, and accident monitoring. Experimental results demonstrate high detection accuracy and real-time performance, highlighting the potential of AI-driven vision systems to significantly improve urban traffic management and safety.
